About Us

  • Serendib Tiles, is a tile factory
  • Over 20 years in business, with over 15 years of export experience
  • The factory is around 380000 square meters, while the show room is around 5000 square meters
  • 15 different hues, over 1000 different designs, and the best kitchen and bathroom tiles.

  • Design, quality, and price are all excellent.
  • The best option for a project and a wholesaler.
Serendib Tiles, based in Colombo, Sri Lanka, is one of the country's largest ceramic tile producers. Our plant has been in operation for over 20 years and employs over 1000 people. The plant covers an area of around 380000 square meters, while the showroom is around 5000 square meters. We have more than 15 years of export experience and sell our products all over the world, including Europe, Australia, Japan, Korea, the Middle East, Russia, the United States, Brazil, and Africa.

We primarily manufacture glazed ceramic wall tiles in the following sizes: 100X300, 200x200, 200x300, 300x450, 300x600, 200x600, 250x600, 270x730, 300x900mm, as well as matching ceramic floor tiles and decor/border for the kitchen, bathroom, and dining room. We have many different color wall tiles, such as Green, Yellow, Orange, Blue, Red, Pink, Purple, and so on, in hundreds of different designs. Color tiles represent vivacity, youth, and creativity. To create the tiles, we have a competent design staff.

We can offer porcelain tiles in three distinct types: polished in a glossy finish, polished in a matt finish, and un-polished in a rough surface, in sizes of 300x300mm, 300x600mm, 500x500mm, 600x600mm, 800x800mm, 1000X1000mm, 600x1200mm, and so on, in a variety of series and colors. Our porcelain tiles have a good quality reputation yet the lowest price in the globe.

We also provide full-polished glazed porcelain tiles and rustic tiles in a variety of sizes, as well as crystal tiles, glass or ceramic mosaic tiles, and TV background cultural tiles. Our tiles are of excellent quality, and we have sold them to a variety of nations throughout the world, including B&Q England, Australia, Russia, China, the Middle East, and a variety of other consumers.

Our Services

Custom Designing

We are always open to new ideas and new designs.Since we are always eager to bring the best products for our valuable customers we are the first tile industry to introduce the custom designing in Sri Lanka.The customer can choose a design and with a special price range we are ready to design the customer chosen design with our well experianced designers.

Glazing

The production method for glazed and unglazed ceramic and porcelain tiles is same. Glazed tiles, on the other hand, go through an extra step in the firing process. Glazed tiles come in a variety of colors and designs, and they're also more stain-resistant than unglazed tiles since they're coated with a non-porous coating of liquid glass.It brings a unique look to the designed tile.We are proud to be the first company to introduce this to our valuable local customers.

Doorstep Delivery

Our adaptability is one of the features that costomers appreciate the most about Cerendib tiles. Whether you're receiving tiles from one of our locations across town or sending them to the opposite side of the nation, we make shipping simple. But flexibility is more than being able to choose any size or distance; it also entails being able to choose when you want the delivery to be made and having it done without having to worry about safety.
